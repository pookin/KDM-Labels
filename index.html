Welcome to the KDM Label Printer!

Search: Type in the search box to find KDM items like Gear, Resources, Disorders, etc.

Suggestions: Click on a suggestion to preview the label.

Print: Click the "Print" button to print the currently previewed label.

Menu (☰):

  • Label Settings: Adjust label dimensions and margins. Save your settings for future use. Click "Show Calibration Label" to print a test label with current dimensions.
  • Content Filters: Select which expansions or philosophies to include/exclude from your search results. Changes are saved automatically.
  • Survivor Sheet: Opens a printable PDF of the KDM: Abyssal Woods Survivor Record Sheet in a new tab.
  • Help: Shows this help dialog.

Note: Ensure your browser's pop-up blocker is not blocking the print window or the Survivor Sheet PDF.

Tip: When printing, ensure your printer settings match the label size defined in "Label Settings" and that any "Scale to Fit" options are turned OFF for accurate sizing.
